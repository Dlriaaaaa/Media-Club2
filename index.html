About Us

Our Mission

Media Club brings together student creators to collect, curate, and showcase original short films and creative projects from across the campus. We cover graphic design, editorial media, and audio-visual production, building a student-driven portfolio platform.

Activities

  • Archive: Collect student short films and projects, archive with permission, and exhibit on our online platform.
  • Design: Campus posters,文创 design, and commissioned commercial design work — all proceeds fund club operations and public-interest projects.
  • Documentary & Interviews: One documentary per semester; monthly interview series featuring campus and city figures.
