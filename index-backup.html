/ 001 About

媒介社

Media Club is a contemporary art and media collective based in Beijing. We explore the intersection of classical aesthetics and digital culture — where Renaissance sculpture meets pixel grids, where darkroom chemistry converges with generative algorithms.

Founded in 2025, our practice spans photography, new media installation, experimental publishing, and cross-disciplinary collaboration. We believe in the radical potential of images — to disrupt, to deconstruct, to re-enchant.
